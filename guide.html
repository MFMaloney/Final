Cooking

Cooking provides many benefits in Black Desert. You can make food to buff your stats, potions to restore your hp, feed for your pets, beer for your workers and more! In addition to a cooking guide you can find a calculator for some commonly used recipes.

First up is beer. You will need beer to keep your workers' stamina up. Enter how much of the grain you have and it will calculate how much of the vendor bought ingredients you will need.
